Xiangxian Li

李象贤

Ph.D. Student

I am now a Ph.D. student and a member of MMRC lab in the School of Software, Shandong University, advised by Prof. Lei Meng and Prof. Xiangxu Meng. My current research revolves around enhancing the unbias learning on imbalanced data by cross-modal learning and causal inference.
